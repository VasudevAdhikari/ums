University of Computer Studies

In 1971, it was founded as UCC, the university Computer Center. In 1988, it was established as an Institute of Computer Science and Technology (ICST). On July 1, 1998, the Institute was changed to the University of Computer Studies, Yangon (UCSY).

Welcome to University of Computer Studies, a beacon of academic excellence and innovation dedicated to fostering future leaders and driving societal progress. Established in 1971, our university has consistently been at the forefront of education, research, and community engagement.

Our institution has grown from a small computer center to a comprehensive university offering cutting-edge programs in computer science, information technology, and related fields.

Our Mission

To provide a transformative educational experience, conduct groundbreaking research, and serve society through knowledge dissemination and creative endeavors in the field of computing and information technology.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution for its pioneering research in computer science, innovative teaching methodologies, and a vibrant community that inspires critical thinking and technological innovation.
